FAQ

Frequently Asked
Questions

How long does a fumigation take?
+

A standard fumigation takes 2–4 hours depending on property size and pest type. We recommend staying out for 3–4 hours after treatment. We'll advise you clearly before we begin.

Are your chemicals safe for children and pets?
+

Yes. We use WHO-approved, eco-friendly products safe for your family and pets once dried — typically within 2–3 hours. Our technician will guide you on re-entry timing.

Do you offer a warranty on your services?
+

All services come with a satisfaction guarantee. If pests return within 30 days, we treat your property again at absolutely no extra cost.

How quickly can you respond to an emergency?
+

We offer same-day emergency service for urgent infestations. Simply WhatsApp or call +254 720 961 362 and we'll dispatch a technician as fast as possible.

Do you serve commercial properties?
+

Yes — restaurants, hotels, warehouses, offices, schools and more. We provide full documentation and compliance reports suitable for health audits and inspections.

How does the referral program work?
+

Refer a friend or neighbour to Pestokil. When they book and mention your name, both of you get KSh 500 off your next treatment. No limits on referrals!
