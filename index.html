FAQ

Frequently Asked
Questions

How long does a fumigation take?
+

A standard fumigation takes 2–4 hours depending on the property size and type of pest. We recommend staying out of the property for 3–4 hours after treatment. We'll advise you clearly before we begin.

Are your chemicals safe for children and pets?
+

Yes. We use WHO-approved, eco-friendly products that are completely safe for your family and pets once dried — typically within 2–3 hours. We will guide you on when it's safe to re-enter your home.

Do you offer a warranty on your services?
+

Absolutely. All our pest control services come with a satisfaction guarantee. If pests return within the warranty period (varies by service type), we will treat your property again at no extra cost.

How quickly can you respond to an emergency?
+

We offer same-day emergency service for urgent infestations. Simply WhatsApp or call us at +254 720 961 362 and we'll dispatch a technician to your location as quickly as possible.

Do you serve commercial properties?
+

Yes. We serve restaurants, hotels, warehouses, offices, schools and more. We provide full documentation and compliance reports suitable for health inspections and audits.

How much does pest control cost?
+

Prices start from KSh 7,000 for a 1-bedroom unit and vary based on property size and infestation level. We offer free inspections — no hidden charges. WhatsApp us for an instant quote.
